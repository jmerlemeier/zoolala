Help penguins! Penguins on the Antarctic Peninsula are suffering the brunt of climate change. When it warms, it rains. When it rains, penguin chicks die. You can help! By insulating your house, reusing items where you can, and carpooling, you can make a difference in the lives of penguin chicks everywhere.
